Privacy Policy

Transfer Clash — Last updated: March 5, 2026

Transfer Clash ("we", "our", or "the app") is a football trivia game developed by mcansener. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, and password when you register an account.
  • Guest Data: A unique device identifier to enable guest play without registration.
  • Game Data: Match history, scores, ELO ratings, win/loss records, and league standings.
  • Preferences: Your preferred language setting.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and operate the game, including matchmaking and leaderboards.
  • Authenticate your identity and maintain your session.
  • Track game statistics and display them on your profile.
  • Enable friend challenges and social features.
  • Improve the app experience and fix issues.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and token-based authentication (JWT) to protect your information. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

The app does not currently integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

5. Children's Privacy

Transfer Clash is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at the email below and we will process your request within a reasonable timeframe.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time by discontinuing use of the app.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
